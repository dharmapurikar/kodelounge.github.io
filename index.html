Product

Kode Lounge is a technology incubator for a US based product company with a mandate to creating disruptive and groundbreaking products for the US healthcare sector. Its very first product product under development replaces an arcane and manual process to create efficiencies, automation and transparency in a sub-sector that is in desperate want for such solution. This presents a unique yet, very challenging opportunity.
